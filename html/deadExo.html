Overview:

In this project, I retrofitted a Sony PSP2000 to replace its outdated barrel jack charging port with a modern USB Type-C connector. This modification was designed to streamline device charging across my electronics collection by adopting a universal charging standard.


Goals:

  • Modenize an older device to use USB-C for Power input
  • Elminate the need for a proprietary charger by enabling charging via common 5V usb sources
  • Apply practical electronics and fabrication skills in a hands-on hardware mod
